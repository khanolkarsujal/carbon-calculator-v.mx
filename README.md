# 🌍 Carbon Calculator V.MX

Carbon Calculator V.MX is a simple web application that helps users **estimate their carbon footprint based on everyday activities** such as transportation, energy usage, and lifestyle habits.

The goal of the project is to **raise awareness about environmental impact** and encourage people to adopt more sustainable habits.

---

# 🎯 Problem

Climate change is strongly influenced by **carbon emissions from daily human activities**.

However, most people:

* Do not understand how their lifestyle affects the environment
* Cannot easily measure their personal carbon footprint
* Lack tools to visualize their environmental impact

---

# 💡 Solution

Carbon Calculator V.MX provides a simple interface where users can:

* Enter lifestyle data
* Calculate estimated CO₂ emissions
* Visualize environmental impact
* Understand which activities produce the most emissions

Carbon footprint calculators estimate emissions by analyzing factors like **transportation, electricity usage, and waste generation**. ([GitHub][1])

---

# ⚙️ Features

* 🌍 Carbon footprint calculator
* 📊 Emission estimation based on user inputs
* 📉 Environmental awareness tool
* 💡 Educational insights on sustainability
* 🌐 Accessible through a web browser

---

# 🚀 How It Works

1. User enters information about their activities
2. The system calculates estimated CO₂ emissions
3. Results show the environmental impact
4. Users can understand which habits contribute most to emissions
